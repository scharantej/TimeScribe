Smart Time Planner - About

This is a simple web application that helps you plan your day by optimizing your schedule based on your tasks, time preferences, and additional information.

To use the application, simply enter your tasks, time slots, and preferences. The application will then generate a schedule that allocates your tasks to specific time slots in an optimized manner.
